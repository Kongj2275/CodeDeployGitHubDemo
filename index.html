Emotion Analyzation

Record your emotional response to your lecturers!

Teachers' and learners' emotions are important because they ultimately affect learning outcomes. However, most research in classroom emotions has been so far focused on emotions in a retrospective way, through the use of interviews or questionnaires. The challenge for researchers is to record emotions in real time.

This web application aims to overcome this methodological issue through recording classroom emotions (teachers or learners) in real time..
